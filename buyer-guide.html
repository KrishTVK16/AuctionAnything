Buyer Guide

Before You Bid

  • Register: Create a free account and verify your identity
  • Research: Study the item details, condition report, and provenance
  • Set Budget: Determine your maximum bid before the auction
  • Check Fees: Review buyer's premium and shipping costs

During the Auction

  • Watch Live: Follow bidding in real-time on our platform
  • Bid Smart: Use automatic bidding to stay competitive
  • Stay Alert: Monitor email and SMS notifications
  • Don't Panic: Stick to your predetermined maximum bid

After Winning

  • Payment: Complete payment within 48 hours of winning
  • Shipping: We handle secure packaging and delivery
  • Insurance: All shipments are fully insured
  • Inspection: Review your item upon delivery

Our Guarantee

  • Authenticity: All items are verified by expert specialists
  • Transparency: Detailed condition reports for every item
  • Security: Secure payments and encrypted transactions
  • Support: 24/7 customer service for registered members
