Returns Policy

Our Commitment

At Ankand, we stand behind the authenticity and quality of every item sold through our platform. While auction sales are generally final, we offer protections for buyers in specific circumstances.

Eligible Returns

Returns are accepted within 14 days of delivery for:

  • Authenticity Issues: Item is determined to be counterfeit
  • Significant Misrepresentation: Item differs materially from listing description
  • Damage in Transit: Item arrived damaged (must report within 48 hours)
  • Wrong Item: Received a different item than purchased

Return Process

  1. Contact our support team within 14 days of delivery
  2. Provide photos and documentation of the issue
  3. Receive a return authorization if eligible
  4. Ship item back in original packaging (prepaid label provided)
  5. Refund processed within 5 business days of receipt

Non-Returnable Items

  • Items accurately described with normal wear for age
  • Items where buyer simply changed their mind
  • Custom or personalized items
  • Items altered after delivery
