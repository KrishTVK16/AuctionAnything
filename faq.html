Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

Ankand is Albania's premier online auction marketplace for luxury items, antiques, fine art, jewelry, and collectors' treasures. We provide a secure platform for buying and selling authentic high-value items.

Click the "Sign Up" button in the navigation bar. Fill in your details including name, email, phone number, and create a secure password. Once registered, you can start bidding on items immediately.

Yes, creating an account and placing bids is completely free. You only pay when you win an auction. A small buyer's premium may apply to successful purchases.

Bidding Process

Navigate to the item you want to bid on, enter your maximum bid amount, and click "Place Bid". Our system will automatically bid on your behalf up to your maximum amount.

Bids are generally binding. However, in exceptional circumstances, you may contact our support team within 1 hour of placing a bid to request a cancellation.

You'll receive an email notification with payment instructions. Complete your payment within 48 hours, and we'll arrange secure delivery of your item.

Payment & Shipping

We accept bank transfers, credit/debit cards (Visa, MasterCard), and secure online payment services. All transactions are encrypted for your security.

Yes, we ship to most countries worldwide. Shipping costs and delivery times vary depending on the destination and item size. See our Shipping Info page for details.
