About Ankand

Our Story

Founded in 2018, Ankand was born from a passion for connecting collectors with extraordinary treasures. What started as a small gallery in Tiranë has grown into Albania's most trusted online auction platform.

Our name "Ankand" reflects our commitment to the time-honored tradition of auctions, while our tagline "Gjeni & Shitet" (Find & Sell) captures the essence of what we do — creating a marketplace where exceptional items find their perfect owners.

Today, we curate hundreds of auctions annually, featuring luxury timepieces, fine art, antique jewelry, classic automobiles, and rare collectibles from across the Balkans and beyond.
